Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper. Click on a square with the left mouse button to reveal it. Any number displayed on a revealed square shows the square's mined neighbor count. If you suspect that a square is mined, right click on it to flag it. Right click on it again to un-flag it. To win, flag all 15 of the mined squares without flagging any non-mined squares.

Reload the page to play again. The game is different every time! Its source code is on Github.
