Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper. Click on a square with the left mouse button to reveal it, and its non-mined neighbors. If there is a number on the square, the number reveals the number of its immediately adjacent neighbors that are mined. If you suspect that a square is mined, right click on it to flag it. Right click on it again to un-flag it, if desired. To win, flag all 12 of the mined squares without flagging any non-mined squares.

Reload the page to play again. The game is different every time!

NOTE: This game currently does not work in the Safari browser.
