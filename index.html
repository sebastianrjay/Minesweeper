Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper. Click on a square with the left mouse button to reveal it, and some of its non-mined neighbors. If there is a number on the square, the number reveals the number of its immediately adjacent neighbors that are mined. If you suspect that a square is mined, right click on it to flag it. Right click on it again to un-flag it, if desired. To win, flag all of the mined tiles without flagging any non-mined tiles.
